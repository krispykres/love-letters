Dear Bash,

We’ve been through a lot together, you know this… but no matter where we were, your ls showed me everything that was right in front of me the whole time. With your guidance, I knew I could just call out for your pwd and I’d never find myself astray. Whether we moved forward together or backtracked, your cd never fails to lead us together. Your gentle touch creates new memories, and your mkdir camera stores them all safely in one place.

I never want our memories to be forgotten, and I’m glad you don’t either. I can scp all our memories onto our private server so, all I need to do is ssh in. You make it so simple to cherish the best times in our lives. I am blessed to be able to talk to you so easily through nano, now we will never have to go a moment without communication. I hope to be able to use your shell forever.

With love,
Ian
